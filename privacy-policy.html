  • No passwords (OAuth only)

  • No payment information
  • No location data beyond IP
  • No browsing history

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Verify your identity via Roblox
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve Service functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We NEVER sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • Roblox Corporation (OAuth provider)

4. Data Security

  • HTTPS/TLS 1.3 for all transmissions
  • IP logs automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Regular security audits
  • Two-factor authentication for admin access
